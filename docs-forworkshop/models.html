ODM2 Models Index¶

ODM2 is organized with a “core” schema and multiple “extension” schemas that extend the functionality of the core. The following sections cover some overarching concepts for ODM2 and then focus on specific entities within the ODM2 Core schema and ODM2’s extension schemas.

ODM2Core Entities¶

The following are entities in the ODM2 Core Schema:

odm2api.ODM2.models.Actions(**kwargs) Actions are performed by people and may have a result.
odm2api.ODM2.models.DataSets(**kwargs) Enables grouping of results into a larger dataset.
odm2api.ODM2.models.FeatureActions(**kwargs) Provides flexible linkage between Actions and the SamplingFeatures on which or at which they were performed.
odm2api.ODM2.models.Methods(**kwargs) The procedure used to perform an action.
odm2api.ODM2.models.Organizations(**kwargs) A group of people.
odm2api.ODM2.models.People(**kwargs) Individuals that perform actions.
odm2api.ODM2.models.ProcessingLevels(**kwargs) Levels to which data have been quality controlled.
odm2api.ODM2.models.RelatedActions(**kwargs) Enables specifying relationships among Actions (e.g., workflows, etc.)
odm2api.ODM2.models.Results(**kwargs) The result of an action.
odm2api.ODM2.models.SamplingFeatures(**kwargs) Where or on what an action was performed.
odm2api.ODM2.models.TaxonomicClassifiers(…) Terms for classifying results.
odm2api.ODM2.models.Units(**kwargs) Units of measure.
odm2api.ODM2.models.Variables(**kwargs) What was observed.
